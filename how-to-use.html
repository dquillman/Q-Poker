3. Advanced Visualization

Opponent Perceived Range

The Range Visualizer shows a 13x13 grid of all possible hands. As the hand progresses, the AI "filters out" hands that the opponent is unlikely to hold based on their betting patterns.

Green squares indicate highly probable hands, while Dark squares represent hands that have been narrowed out.

Practice specific high-pressure scenarios in Drill Mode, or sharpen your mental math in the Math Dojo. Estimate your equity against random hands to build the intuition needed for professional-level play.
